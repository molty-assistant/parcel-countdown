## Android (Google Play Console)

**Title** (max 30 chars):
Parcel Countdown

**Short Description** (max 80 chars):
Beautiful countdown timers for every parcel and delivery you're waiting for.

**Full Description** (max 4000 chars):
Waiting for a parcel? Parcel Countdown gives you a beautiful, satisfying countdown to every delivery you're expecting.

Add your parcels with an expected delivery date and watch the days, hours, and minutes tick down in real time. No sign-ups, no tracking numbers to enter, no complicated setup — just a simple countdown to the things you're looking forward to.

FEATURES

★ Real-time countdown — see days, hours, and minutes until each delivery
★ Add unlimited parcels with names, expected dates, and optional notes
★ Save tracking links for quick access to your carrier's website
★ Delivered parcels are marked with a satisfying green checkmark
★ Smart sorting — nearest deliveries are always shown first
★ Haptic feedback on every interaction for a satisfying tactile experience
★ Supports both light and dark mode automatically
★ 100% offline — all your data stays on your device, always
★ No ads, no analytics, no data collection of any kind

HOW IT WORKS

1. Tap the + button to add a new parcel
2. Enter a name and expected delivery date
3. Optionally add a tracking URL and notes
4. Watch the countdown tick down in real time
5. Once the delivery date passes, it's marked as delivered

PRIVACY FIRST

Parcel Countdown stores everything locally on your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no data collection. Your delivery information never leaves your phone.

Perfect for online shoppers, small business owners awaiting stock, or anyone who just loves the anticipation of a delivery.
